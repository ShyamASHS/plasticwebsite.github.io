Plastic Pollution

Plastic pollution is an overgrowing issue. This is where plastic is thrown around by the community and ends up on our beaches and oceans. Plastic on the beaches is an awful thing as that plastic will eventually end up inside the water.

Effects on Marine Life

When it ends up in the ocean and the water, marine life is affected. This is because marine life thinks plastic is food, and plastic is actually harmful for them. This results in marine life being endangered and certain types of fish can actually go extinct, in the future, if plastic pollution is not dealt with.

Statistics

Here are some statistics: Here are some statistics: Daily, almost 8 million pieces of plastic pollution is found around our oceans. 12 million tonnes of plastic are dumped into the ocean every year. Scientists have recently discovered microplastics that are deep in the Arctic ice. Plastics make up 80% of all marine debris studied.
