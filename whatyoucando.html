What You Can Do

Here is what you can do


We have learnt a lot about the issue so far and if you want,
There is also things that you can do to help contribute to this issue and reduce it. Firstly, you could reach out to big non government organizations and try to get in touch with them, offering them your help, in contributing to this issue. Another thing you can do is donate to the much smaller plastic campaigns that are around the world trying to grab attention. Those guys are doing a great job and you donating to them will really help contribute to this issue. Overall, I think even a bit of help or you picking up 2 or 3 pieces of plastic when you next visit the beach can make an impact, no matter the size.
